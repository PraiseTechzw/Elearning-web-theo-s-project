Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect to the campus Wi-Fi?

Select "CUT-Student" or "CUT-Staff" network, enter your student/staff credentials, and accept the terms of service.

What should I do if I can't access my email?

Check your internet connection first, then try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. Contact IT support if the issue persists.

How do I reset my password?

Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password" or contact the IT helpdesk for assistance with password reset.

What software is available for students?

Students have access to Microsoft Office 365, various programming tools, and specialized software for their courses through the university portal.
